About Us

Santa Report Card was started by Rick and Joe - two dads in Ohio. Our Children are grown, but we often reflect about the Christmas memories we had as children and as parents. We want others to have those same magical memories, we also have a strong desire to help parents raise children who are confident, respectful and productive members of society. Lastly, we want to help those who are less fortunate. So, all of that together led to an idea; and that idea culminated in Santa Report card in January of 2025. Our Goals

  • Help instill the magic of Christmas year-round and prolong the magic of Santa within children.
  • Help parents achieve the specific behavior and values they desire in their children.
  • Help children become confident, responsible, disciplined and productive.
  • Help ensure that less fortunate families have presents from Santa.
  • Help improve society by developing children into adults who are effective and successful contributors and leaders.
