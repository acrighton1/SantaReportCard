What Is Santa Report Card?

The Santa Report Card is a unique and engaging tool designed to help parents encourage positive behavior and development in their children throughout the year. With this innovative concept, parents can choose specific criteria on which their children will be graded monthly, culminating in a personalized report card from Santa Claus himself. This approach not only motivates children to improve their behavior but also fosters a strong sense of confidence, responsibility and accomplishment. Lastly, the Santa Report Card is a great way to instill the magic of Christmas year-round and help keep the magic within children as long as possible!

Features of the Santa Report Card

Monthly Grading

Each month, you can assess your child's performance based on the selected criteria. This regular evaluation helps children understand the importance of consistency and effort in achieving their goals. It also allows you to determine their grade for each area and specific examples for each one.

Personalized Report Card

At the end of each month, your child will receive a beautifully designed report card from Santa Claus, complete with their grades and encouraging comments. This personal touch adds a magical element to the experience, making it both fun and motivating.

Progress Tracking

The Santa Report Card system includes a progress-tracking feature that allows you and your child to monitor improvements over time. This feature provides valuable insights into areas where children excel and where they may need additional support.

Positive Reinforcement

The Santa Report Card emphasizes positive reinforcement, focusing on the child's achievements and efforts rather than solely on their shortcomings. This approach helps build self-esteem and encourages continuous improvement.

Customizable Criteria

You have the flexibility to select from over 50 different criteria that align with your child's developmental goals and household values. These criteria include things such as:

  • Chores and Responsibilities
  • Academic Performance
  • Social Skills and Friendships
  • Manners and Respect
  • Personal Hygiene
  • Time Management
  • Healthy Habits
  • Religious
  • Creativity and Hobbies

FAQs

Here are some of the most common questions we received. If your question isn't listed, reach out and we'll get back to you!

Do Kids Get Access to the System?

The back end of the system, where grades are entered, is designed for parents. We don't collect any information about your children except their first and last name. We also collect minimal information from the parent. So while you may certainly allow the children to view the web site or parts of it, the intent is for parents to be the primary user.

Can I select my own criteria?

We currently have over 50 different criteria to choose from. Additional criteria will be added in the future. Send us a note if you have ideas for other criteria.

Can I change the criteria at any time?

Yes, you can choose from any of the various criteria from month to month. Keep in mind that it is best to keep them the same for at least 3 months to build positive behavior and get the best results.

How Does the Grading Work?

It's intentionally very simple. You select a monthly grade for each of the 5 criteria that you selected when registering. The grade is one of three options: Green = Great Job; Yellow = Good Job; and Red = Needs Work. You can also add specific comments to support each of those grades. For example, if you gave a Yellow for keeping your room clean, you could say something such as, "Good job overall. Remember to pick up those dirty clothes in the closet".

How is the Grade Determined?

You have complete control over the monthly grade that is given as well as the overall grade. There are no complicated calculations in the background. This allows you flexibility and freedom in choosing the overall grade for the month. For example, 3 green, 1 red and 1 yellow might be a green to one parent and yellow to another. The choice is yours!

How many children can I register?

You can register as any children that are part of your direct family. Please no sharing of accounts with others. Anyone who is caught doing so will lose their membership without a refund.

How do I get the actual report card?

It's simple! Once you've entered your grades for the month, you simply select a button to finalize the report card and print it. You can also email or forward it and save it. All previous report cards are also available for you!

How does it all work from start to finish?

  • Register for an account.
  • Add your child(ren) to your account (first and last name is all we need).
  • Select the 5 attributes for each child (yes, they can be different per child). Make sure that you share the attributes/expectations with your children, so they know what is expected.
  • Once per month, grade each child on each attribute based on that month's performance. Add specific comments/examples for each one. Then provide the overall score for the month.
  • Finalize and print the report card.
  • Review the report card with each child to explain how they did. Celebrate the positives and work on way to improve the other areas.
