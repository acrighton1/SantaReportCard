How Santa Report Card Works

At Santa Report Card, we've created a fun, effective, and magical way to help parents encourage positive behavior and development in their children throughout the year. Here's how it works:

Step 1: Register for an Account

Start by signing up for a Santa Report Card account. It's quick, easy, and affordable!

Step 2: Add Your Child(ren)

Once registered, add your children's names to your account to start creating personalized report cards.

Step 3: Choose Your Criteria

Pick from over 50 criteria to tailor each child's report card to their unique needs and goals.

Step 4: Grading Your Child

Each month, grade your child's performance using a simple Green, Yellow, Red system.

Step 5: Track Progress

Track your child's progress and see where they excel and where they need improvement.

Step 6: Get the Report Card from Santa

Receive a beautifully designed report card with Santa's personalized feedback!

Step 7: Celebrate and Improve

Celebrate your child's growth and set new goals for the next month.

Additional Features

Customizable Criteria

Change your child's grading criteria each month to suit their developmental needs and goals.

Multiple Children

You can register unlimited children under one account, allowing you to manage all your kids' progress in one place.

Positive Reinforcement

Santa's feedback isn't just about grades; it's about encouraging growth and building confidence.

Charitable Giving

A portion of every membership goes toward helping less fortunate families experience the magic of Christmas.
